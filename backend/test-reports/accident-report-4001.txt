ACCIDENT INVESTIGATION REPORT

1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

On February 13, 2026, a Silver Bay Seafoods delivery truck (Unit #12) was rear-ended while stopped at a red light on I-5 southbound in Seattle. The company vehicle, a Ford F-550 box truck, sustained minor damage to the rear bumper and taillight assembly. Fortunately, the driver, Kevin Anderson, was uninjured in the collision. The other driver involved was cited by police for following too closely.

2. CAUSATION ANALYSIS

The primary cause of this accident was the other driver's failure to maintain a safe following distance. According to the police report, the private vehicle collided with the rear of the Silver Bay Seafoods truck while it was stopped at a red light. This indicates the other driver was not providing enough braking distance, a violation of basic traffic safety principles.

Contributing factors to the accident include:

- Environmental Conditions: The incident occurred during daylight hours with clear visibility. Weather conditions were dry, presenting no adverse environmental factors.
- Human Factors: The other driver's inattention and lack of safe driving practices directly led to the rear-end collision. No evidence suggests impairment or distraction of the Silver Bay Seafoods driver.

3. COMPLIANCE & LIABILITY

A review of the relevant regulations and company policies shows that Silver Bay Seafoods was in full compliance at the time of the accident:

- Department of Transportation (DOT) regulations: The delivery truck and driver were operating within all applicable DOT safety standards.
- Company policies: Kevin Anderson, the Silver Bay Seafoods driver, was following all internal fleet safety protocols.

Based on the police report and findings, the liability for this accident primarily rests with the other driver who was cited for following too closely. Silver Bay Seafoods and its employee, Kevin Anderson, are not deemed liable for this incident.

4. CORRECTIVE ACTIONS

While the Silver Bay Seafoods driver was not at fault, the company will take the following steps to address this incident:

- Driver Retraining: Kevin Anderson will undergo refresher training on defensive driving techniques to further enhance his safe driving practices.
- Vehicle Maintenance: The delivery truck's rear bumper and taillight assembly will be repaired to restore the vehicle to full working order.

5. PREVENTION MEASURES

To help prevent future rear-end collisions involving Silver Bay Seafoods vehicles, the following safety improvements will be implemented:

- Fleet Safety Upgrades: Installation of dashboard cameras and advanced telematics systems in all company trucks to monitor driver behavior and provide valuable data for safety analysis.
- Additional Driver Training: Expansion of defensive driving courses and safety awareness programs for all Silver Bay Seafoods commercial vehicle operators.

6. CLAIM MANAGEMENT

Silver Bay Seafoods has notified its insurance provider of the accident, and all relevant documentation, including the police report and this investigation assessment, will be submitted to support the claim process.

In conclusion, this rear-end collision was the result of the other driver's failure to maintain a safe following distance. Silver Bay Seafoods has taken appropriate steps to address the incident, ensure compliance, and implement additional safety measures to prevent similar occurrences in the future.
